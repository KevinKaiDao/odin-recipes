Caramel Custard Pudding [Nino's Home]

Description

This is my goto Flan recipe, its very easy and very simple to make. You can also adjust the sweetness to however sweet you like it.

Ingredients

  • 80g Sugar
  • 15ml Water
  • 3 Egg yolks
  • 3 Eggs
  • 500ml Milk
  • 90g Sugar
  • 200g Heavy cream
  • 1Tsp Vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Combine 80g of sugar with 15ml of water in a stove.
  2. Then put on medium heat until sugar turns brown.
  3. Pour caramel into ramekins.
  4. Combine 3 eggs and 3 egg yolks into a seperate bowl and wisk lightly until fully combined.
  5. In a seperate pot, add 500ml milk, 90g sugar, and 200g heavy cream. Heat it up until its hot around the rims.
  6. Slowly pour milk mixture into the egg mixture while stirring.
  7. Strain the egg/milk mixture into a seperate container through a mesh filter. Then pour into ramekins.
  8. Put the ramekins into a hot water bath with boiling water only half way up the ramekins.
  9. Bake in an oven at 150 degrees celcius for 50 minutes.
  10. Let it cool down a bit and fridge.
